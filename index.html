What services we provide:

Social & Emotional Growth

Empower children to build meaningful relationships, understand emotions, and foster social skills through interactive activities and group play.

Physical Development

Enhance gross and fine motor skills with engaging exercises and fun physical activities designed for all levels of growth.

Literacy Exploration

Inspire a love for reading and writing with pre-reading and pre-writing activities tailored to develop foundational literacy skills.

Language Development

Cultivate expressive and receptive language skills through storytime, group discussions, and engaging communication games.

Cognitive Skills Development

Stimulate young minds with activities that encourage problem-solving, logical reasoning, and an early understanding of mathematical concepts.

Creative curriculum

Children learn through exploratory and active play, guided by trained teachers who plan activities tailored to each child’s developmental level. The curriculum is available in English, French, and German based on parents’ preferences.
  • Social & emotional development
  • Physical development (gross & fine motor skills)
  • Language development (expressive & receptive)
  • Literacy (pre-reading & pre-writing )
  • Cognitive Development (pre- math)

Center Based Learning

We have adopted a successful "center-based" learning approach, rooted in the theory that children learn best through play. Each class features the following centers:
  • Games & Puzzles Center – for table games, puzzles, visual discrimination, and threading.
  • Reading & Listening Center – teaching them to enjoy reading and listening to stories, as well as providing them with many pre-reading stimulation.
  • Dramatic Center – teaching children to take on a role and engage in imitative behavior and make-believe play.
  • Block & Construction Center – for playing with blocks, Lego, and Mechano.
  • Creative Center – teaching as well as enhancing talents through painting, coloring, play dough, cutting and pasting.
  • Writing Center – to teach pre-writing and writing skills as well as enhance their fine motor and manual dexterity.
  • Math & Science Center – for experimentation with and learning about numbers, counting, weights, nature exploration, etc.

Provision for All Ages

We group children by age and preferred language (English, French, or German), tailoring activities to their developmental needs:
  • Infants (3–15 months): Focus on sensory exploration, messy play, music, and movement with individualized care for up to 9 infants.
  • Toddlers (15–24 months): Encourage mobility, language, and independence through group activities like Choice Time, Circle Time, and outdoor play for up to 12 toddlers
  • Juniors (2–3 years): Develop social and basic skills through purposeful play, Planning and Recall Times, and creative activities for up to 15 children.
  • Seniors & Pre-Schoolers (3–5 years): Prepare for school with structured learning, group activities, and skill-building for up to 18–20 children.

Our Learning Environment

Our classrooms are clean, safe, and equipped with materials like puzzles, natural items, and recycled objects, fostering exploration in a hygienic environment. Outdoor spaces also offer diverse toys, encouraging learning through play with health and safety prioritized.

Inclusion

We strive to meet each child's individual needs, including those with physical disabilities or learning challenges, while ensuring all children thrive. Admission is based on individual circumstances and our ability to provide appropriate care. Collaboration with parents is key to ensuring every child's well-being.
