The Creative Curriculum

An internationally recognized, tried, tested, and constantly updated curriculum designed specifically for children from birth to 5 years of age.

Infants

Age: 3 months to 1 year

A nurturing environment for sensory play, music, and early development activities.

Toddlers

Age: 1 to 2 years

Fostering mobility, language, and hands-on activities like messy play and outdoor exploration.

Juniors

Age: 2 to 3 years

Building confidence, social skills, and cognitive abilities through purposeful play and structured group time.

Preschoolers

Age: 4 to 5 years

Preparing children for school with pre-reading, pre-math, and interactive learning experiences.

Main Areas of Development

We focus on key areas to ensure holistic development for young learners.

Social & Emotional Development

Helping children build confidence, manage emotions, and establish positive relationships.

Physical Development

(Gross & Fine Motor Skills) Enhancing coordination, strength, and overall physical growth.

Language Development

(Expressive & Receptive) Developing communication skills through listening and speaking activities.

Literacy

(Pre-reading & Pre-writing) Encouraging foundational skills for reading and writing through interactive activities.

Cognitive Development

(Pre-math) Strengthening problem-solving and critical-thinking skills through hands-on learning.

Creative Development

Fostering imagination and self-expression through artistic activities and play.

Learning Centers

Our center-based learning approach ensures children engage in play-based, hands-on activities across various disciplines.

Games & Puzzles Center

Encourages problem-solving and critical-thinking skills through interactive games.

Reading & Listening Center

Develops literacy through books, storytelling, and listening exercises.

Dramatic Center

Fosters creativity and social interaction through pretend play.

Block & Construction Center

Teaches spatial awareness and engineering concepts through building activities.

Creative Center

Encourages self-expression through art, craft, and imaginative play.

Writing Center

Supports literacy development through writing exercises and activities.

The Baby Academy is dedicated to providing children with a safe, loving, and stimulating environment where they can grow, learn, and thrive. Our mission is to nurture every child’s potential through play and exploration.
